Jauhelihaperunasoselaatikko

Very good stuff

  • 400 g of ground beef or pork
  • 10 pcs of potatoes
  • 75g of butter
  • 0.5 teaspoon of salt
  • 3-4 dl of milk
  • one onion
  • a tablespoon of canola oil
  • a teaspoon of ground paprika
  • black pepper
  • 2 eggs

Peel and slice the potatoes. Boil them in water with salt. Pour the water away and mash the potatoes.

Pour some milk to the potatoes until they get smooth. Add butter and salt.

Peel and slice the onion. Fry it in a pan with oil. Add the ground mea to the pan and fry until it's done. Season with salt, paprika and pepper. Mix the onion-meat-stuff in to the smashed potatoes and add the eggs.

Pour everything in to a baking tray. Cook it in an oven's middle section in 200 degrees for 45 minutes

Serve with lingonberry jam and pickled cucumber
